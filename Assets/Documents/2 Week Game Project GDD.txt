Game  Summary

escalating

Core  Fantasy  
●  A  lone  operative  dropped  into  a  hostile  zone.  ●  Survive  long  enough  to  extract.  ●  Every  run  is  a  gamble:  risk  death  and  lose  everything,  or  extract  early  and  bank  rewards.  
Core  Pillars  
●  Fast,  responsive  combat  ●  Short,  addictive  runs  ●  High ‑ risk  extraction  tension  ●  Simple  but  meaningful  progression  ●  Minimal  content,  maximum  replayability  
 
2.  Gameplay  Loop  
Moment ‑ to ‑ Moment  Loop  
1.  Drop  in  2.  Fight  waves  3.  Survive  until  extraction  appears  4.  Reach  extraction  zone  5.  Hold  for  5  seconds  6.  Extract  and  earn  currency  7.  Buy  upgrades  8.  Repeat  

Run  Duration  
●  Three  minutes  until  extraction  appears  ●  Five ‑ second  extraction  channel  ●  Optional:  stay  longer  for  bonus  currency  
Failure  Condition  
●  Death  results  in  losing  all  run  currency  
 
3.  Player  Systems  
Movement  
●  WASD  movement  ●  Smooth  acceleration  ●  Top ‑ down  camera  follow  
Weapons  
All  weapons  are  hitscan.  
●  Pistol:  balanced  and  accurate  ●  Shotgun:  close ‑ range  burst  ●  SMG:  high  fire  rate,  low  accuracy  
Shared  assets:  
●  Muzzle  flash  ●  Bullet  impact  ●  Sound  effects  ●  Recoil  animation  
Health  System  
●  Fixed  player  health  ●  No  healing  during  runs  ●  Damage  from  enemy  contact  
 
4.  Enemy  Systems  

Enemy  Types  
Basic  Chaser  
●  Moves  directly  toward  the  player  ●  Deals  contact  damage  ●  Low  health  ●  High  spawn  rate  
Elite  Chaser  
●  Larger  and  faster  ●  Higher  health  ●  Spawns  periodically  
AI  Behavior  
●  Simple  seek  behavior  ●  No  pathfinding  
Spawner  
●  Spawns  enemies  in  rings  around  the  arena  ●  Spawn  rate  increases  every  30  seconds  ●  Spawn  cap  to  maintain  performance  
 
5.  Extraction  System  
Extraction  Trigger  
●  Extraction  zone  appears  at  the  three ‑ minute  mark.  
Extraction  Zone  
●  Circular  glowing  area  ●  Player  must  stand  inside  for  five  seconds  ●  Taking  damage  resets  the  timer  
Rewards  
●  Currency  awarded  only  on  successful  extraction  

 
6.  Meta  Progression  
Currency  
●  Earned  only  on  extraction  ●  Used  to  unlock  permanent  upgrades  
Upgrade  Tree  (Six  Nodes)  
●  Damage  +10%  ●  Fire  Rate  +10%  ●  Move  Speed  +10%  ●  Dash  Charge  +1  ●  Extraction  Bonus  +10%  ●  Pickup  Radius  +10%  
Upgrade  Screen  
●  Single  page  with  simple  buttons  
 
7.  UI  and  UX  
HUD  
●  Health  bar  ●  Ammo  count  ●  Timer  ●  Extraction  indicator  
Menus  
●  Main  Menu:  Play,  Upgrades,  Quit  ●  Pause  Menu:  Resume,  Quit  ●  Run  Summary:  Currency  gained  and  upgrades  available  
 
8.  Art  Direction  

Style:  Low ‑ Poly  Hard ‑ Shadow  Minimalism  
●  Flat  colors  ●  Simple  shapes  ●  Hard  shadows  ●  Minimal  textures  ●  High  contrast  readability  
Color  Palette  
●  Arena:  dark  navy  or  charcoal  ●  Player:  cyan  or  white  ●  Enemies:  red  ●  Extraction:  gold  
Visual  Effects  
●  Additive  muzzle  flash  ●  White  hit  flash  ●  Red  enemy  pop  ●  Simple  circles  for  impacts  
 
9.  Audio  Direction
